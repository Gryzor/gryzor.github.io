About my Microphone

What microphone do you have?

A Blue Yeti USB hanging from a Rode Arm, and the RADIUS III mount. I do not remember what the Pop filters are since I bought them years ago; there is nothing special about them.

I may have an older version of the "mount" since I brought it circa 2014 (if I correctly recall) but it looks very similar and if the newer has new features, I don't know what they are.

Are you a "content creator, youtuber, streamer, podcaster, etc." or do you record vocals with it?

No and no; I have recorded vocals in the past with it, it's "ok". (not mine)

What do you use this setup for?

Work calls (Slack, Teams).

Is it good?

Yes, it has a clear sound (as most condenser mics do) but it does pick ambient noise (whether it's voices, or mechanical keyboard clacks, or even a light tap on your desktop), so if you're in a noisy environment, I recommend you look for a different type of microphone.
